About Us

Nile Cargo Shippping LLC was founded by two long time friends and visionary entrepreneurs, one from Mali “Bakary Tambadou” and the other from Uganda “Yiki Vincent ” United by their shared experiences as immigrants and their deep understanding of the challenges faced by African entrepreneurs, they established Nile Cargo Shipping to bridge the gap between Africa and the American economy through innovative transport and logistics solutions.

  • Bridge the gap between African and American Economies

  • Innovative transport and logistics solutions

Mission

At NileCargo Shipping, our mission is to empower African entrepreneurs by providing efficient, reliable, and affordable shipping services. We understand that access to global markets is crucial for the growth of business in Africa, and we are dedicated to facilitating this access through our comprehensive logistics services.

Vision

Nile Cargo Shipping envisions a future where African businesses thrive in the global marketplace. By leveraging our extensive network and expertise, we aim to create opportunities for entrepreneurs to connect with American consumers and partners, Ultimately fostering economic growth and innovation. Just a logistics company; we are partners in progress.Wheather you’re an African entrepreneur looking to expand your business or a partner seeking reliable shipping solutions, we invite you to join on this journey. Together, we can build bridges between continents and create a prosperous future for all.
